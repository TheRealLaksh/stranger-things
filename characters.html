The Heroes of Hawkins

Faces of the resistance against the Upside Down. As the final battle approaches, lines are drawn, and no one is safe.

Eleven

The Mage

Born Jane Hopper, "El" is the world's only defense against Vecna. Having reclaimed her powers, she now struggles with the weight of destiny. In the final war, she must push her psychic abilities beyond their limit to protect her friends and close the gates forever—even if it costs her everything.

Will Byers

The Spy

The boy who survived. Will’s connection to the Upside Down has evolved from a curse into a weapon. As Vecna grows stronger, Will can sense his presence and thoughts. Now coming of age and grappling with his identity, he is no longer the victim—he is the key to destroying the Hive Mind.

Vecna

The General

Formerly Henry Creel (001), he is the architect of all pain in Hawkins. Severely wounded but not defeated, he retreats to the shadows to heal and evolve. His final plan involves merging the dimensions completely, using the children of Hawkins as fuel for his new world order.

Mike Wheeler

The Paladin

The heart of the Party. Mike’s leadership and unwavering belief in Eleven have held the group together since the beginning. As the stakes rise, he must step out of El's shadow to lead the ground war in Hawkins, proving that courage is just as powerful as telekinesis.

Jim Hopper

The Chief

Having survived a Russian gulag and the Demogorgon, Hopper returns to a Hawkins under siege. Hardened but hopeful, he is fiercely protective of El and Joyce. He leads the town's physical defense, utilizing heavy weaponry and police tactics against supernatural horrors.

Joyce Byers

The Mother

From Christmas lights to Russian prisons, Joyce will stop at nothing to save her family. Her intuition and resilience are unmatched. In Season 5, she acts as the tactical anchor for the adults, ensuring that Will and Eleven have the emotional ground they need to fight.

Max Mayfield

The Zoomer

Left comatose and blind after her duel with Vecna, Max’s body lies in a hospital bed, but her mind is trapped elsewhere. She is currently a prisoner in Vecna’s mindscape ("Camazotz"). Her rescue is the group’s primary emotional mission—and possibly the key to Vecna’s weakness.

Lucas Sinclair

The Ranger

Guilt-ridden over Max’s condition, Lucas has hardened into a warrior. No longer just the skeptic, he is a field leader. He stands guard at the hospital while coordinating with the party, driven by a singular promise: to wake Max up, no matter the cost.

Dustin Henderson

The Bard

The brains of the operation. Still grieving the tragic loss of Eddie Munson, Dustin channels his pain into solving the mysteries of the Upside Down. His intellect, radio tech skills, and unbreakable bond with Steve Harrington are vital assets in the final war.

Steve Harrington

The Babysitter

From King Steve to the world's best babysitter. Steve throws himself into danger to protect the "nuggets." wielding his nail-bat with reckless bravery. In the final season, he seeks to find his own purpose beyond protection, deepening his friendships with Dustin and Robin.

Nancy Wheeler

The Investigator

A journalist with perfect aim. Nancy uncovers the darkest secrets of Hawkins Lab and Vecna’s past. Armed with a shotgun and a notepad, she leads the investigation into Vecna’s new plan, often putting herself on the front lines alongside Jonathan and Steve.

Jonathan Byers

The Protector

Will’s brother and Nancy’s partner. Jonathan has always been the quiet observer, but the threat to his family forces him into action. He struggles to balance his dreams of college with the nightmare in Hawkins, serving as the steady hand when chaos erupts.

Robin Buckley

The Strategist

Steve's platonic soulmate. Robin’s frantic energy hides a brilliant mind capable of decoding enemy ciphers and plans. She provides crucial emotional support and comic relief, proving that bravery doesn't mean you aren't terrified.

Erica Sinclair

Lady Applejack

You can't spell America without Erica. Lucas’s younger sister has graduated from comic relief to a critical tactician. Unafraid to speak truth to power (or monsters), she leads the younger generation into battle with sass and surprising courage.

NEW THREAT

Holly Wheeler

The Target

Mike and Nancy's youngest sister. Formerly a background player, Holly begins exhibiting strange behaviors and "vanishing" episodes. She is becoming a primary target for Vecna’s influence, dragging the Wheeler family directly into the eye of the storm.

NEW VILLAIN

Dr. Kay

The Scientist

A ruthless government scientist picking up where Brenner left off. Dr. Kay is hunting Eleven, viewing her not as a human, but as a biological weapon to be controlled. She represents the human evil that complicates the war against the Upside Down.

Murray Bauman

The Conspiracy Theorist

Black belt in karate, fluent in Russian, and paranoid about everything. Murray provides the safehouses and the crazy plans. Despite his eccentricities, his intel is often the only thing keeping the government off the Party's back.

Karen Wheeler

The Matriarch

For years she was oblivious, but now the truth is on her doorstep. With Holly targeted and her town crumbling, Karen finally steps up to defend her children, proving she has the same fire as Nancy and Mike.
